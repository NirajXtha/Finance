Manjushree is a Class 'C' Financial Institution licensed by Nepal Rastra Bank, established in 28 Ashoj 2064 as a National Level Finance Company. Our vision is to become the country’s premier financial institution known for its expertise and services in providing superior financial solutions to you.

Manjushree is equipped with a strong capital base, a team of competent and dedicated management professionals, strong IT infrastructure and systems, wider and deeper connections in the market, Manjushree now stands as a very modern and efficient financial institution. It provides customer-focused financial services to you in a very friendly environment.

Promoted by a team of eminent people with long experience and reputation in banking, business, government, education and development sectors, Manjushree is committed to lead in meeting your financial needs and expectations. It optimizes all its strengths, expertise, advice and services in a very flexible and effective manner in serving your financial needs.

A team of young and dynamic professionals with a very successful track record in banking, marketing, and IT is responsible for the management of the company. Each and every professional at Manjushree is dedicated to helping you achieve your goals With the paid-up capital of NRs. 965 million, Manjushree today is one of the largest finance companies in the country.

Vision

Our vision is to become a premier financial institution known for its expertise and services in providing superior financial solutions to our customers

Mission

We are committed to helping our customers achieve their financial goals through innovative and superior financial solutions. In doing so, we will optimize all the resources provided by our customers, promoters, investors, employees, suppliers, local and business communities, and regulatory bodies to meet the expectations of all our stakeholders.

Core Values

To realize our mission and goals, each member of the Manjushree family shares the following values and responsibilities

Our Customers

We are deeply committed to meeting the needs and expectations of our customers and constantly focus on customer satisfaction. We can prosper only by anticipating and fulfilling customer needs, responding quickly to changing conditions, and meeting customer expectations better than our competitors.

Our Commitment to Quality

At Manjushree, we apply total quality concepts to our products, services, processes, our marketing efforts, our people, and our relationships with each other.

Our Team Members

We believe in people as a competitive advantage. We are responsible for our team members who work with us. We maintain an environment that encourages personal growth and responsibility within the company and community.

Our Corporate Governance

At Manjushree, corporate governance is everyone’s responsibility. We are committed to best practices, ethics, and transparency in business, and comply with all rules and regulations.

Our Community

As a company and as individuals, we play an active role in making every community we serve a better place to live and work.

Our Stockholders

We are ultimately responsible and accountable to our valued shareholders. We will honor them with a fair return on their investment and recognition for their contribution.
